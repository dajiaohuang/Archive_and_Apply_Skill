# Role-Specific CV Audit

Use this reference when the user wants to tailor a CV for different role directions.

## Goal

Do not audit only which project bullets to keep. Also audit:

- which source entries should appear
- which should be shortened, weakened, or dropped
- which items belong in the Skills section
- which items should stay inside project bullets instead of entering Skills

### 14. Graduate & Professional School Applications / 硕博申请

Use this section when the user is applying to graduate programs, professional schools, or fellowships. Tailor the CV to highlight research potential, academic fit, and the specific program type.

#### 14.1 Doctoral Programs / 博士项目

**PhD (Doctor of Philosophy)**
- CS / ECE / EE / Statistics / Applied Math
- Mechanical / Civil / Chemical / Materials Engineering
- Physics / Chemistry / Biology / Environmental Science
- Economics / Psychology / Sociology / Political Science
- Humanities (History / Philosophy / Literature / Linguistics)

**Key focus:** research experience, publications, recommendation letters, research statement, fit with advisor/lab.

**EdD (Doctor of Education)** — practice-oriented doctorate in education.

**DPhil (Doctor of Philosophy)** — Oxford/Cambridge equivalent of PhD.

#### 14.2 Master's Programs — Research / 研究型硕士

**MS / MA / MSc (Master of Science / Arts)**
- Terminal research-oriented master's, often a stepping stone to PhD
- Common in: CS, Statistics, Economics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Linguistics, Psychology
- Key focus: coursework strength, research projects, thesis, advisor connection.

**MPhil (Master of Philosophy)** — research master's, common in UK/Asia (Hong Kong, Singapore). Often a PhD pathway.

**MRes (Master of Research)** — intensive research training master's, common in UK. Strong preparation for PhD.

**Meng (Master of Engineering)** — research-oriented engineering master's with thesis component.

#### 14.3 Master's Programs — Professional / 职业型硕士

**MBA (Master of Business Administration)**
- Full-time / Part-time / Executive / Online
- General Management / Finance / Marketing / Operations / Strategy concentration
- Key focus: leadership experience, career narrative, extracurriculars, GMAT/GRE, interview performance.

**MFin / MSF (Master of Finance)**
- Quantitative Finance / Corporate Finance / FinTech concentration
- Key focus: math/finance background, CFA readiness, internship experience.

**MSBA / MMA (Master of Science in Business Analytics / Analytics)**
- Data-driven business decision making
- Key focus: technical skills (SQL, Python, ML), business communication, project work.

**MSM / MIM (Master of Science in Management / International Management)**
- For early-career applicants without significant work experience.

**MAcc (Master of Accounting)**
- CPA exam preparation pathway.
- Key focus: accounting coursework, attention to detail, professional exposure.

#### 14.4 STEM Master's Programs / STEM 硕士

Many non-engineering programs carry STEM designation (qualifying for extended OPT in the US):

- Data Science / Data Engineering
- Business Analytics / Marketing Analytics
- Financial Engineering / Quantitative Finance
- Information Systems / Information Science
- Bioinformatics / Computational Biology
- Environmental Engineering / Sustainability
- Statistics / Applied Mathematics
- Management Science & Engineering
- Operations Research

#### 14.5 Law & Policy / 法律政策

**JD (Juris Doctor)** — US law school. Key focus: LSAT, analytical writing, diverse perspectives, public interest or corporate narrative.

**LLM (Master of Laws)** — specialized postgraduate law. Common tracks: US Law, International Law, IP Law, Tax Law, Corporate Law.

**MPA (Master of Public Administration)** — government/nonprofit management. Key focus: policy analysis, leadership, public service motivation.

**MPP (Master of Public Policy)** — policy research and analysis. Key focus: quantitative policy skills, sector knowledge.

**MCL / LLM (Master of Comparative Law)** — for international lawyers.

#### 14.6 Medicine & Health / 医学健康

**MD (Doctor of Medicine)** — US/Canada medical school. Key focus: clinical experience (shadowing, volunteering), research, MCAT, service orientation.

**MPH (Master of Public Health)** — population health, epidemiology, health policy.
- Key focus: public health experience, data skills, global health interest.

**MSW (Master of Social Work)** — clinical or community social work practice.

**MHA (Master of Health Administration)** — healthcare management and leadership.

**Nursing / Physician Assistant / PT / OT programs** — clinical practice pathways.

#### 14.7 Arts, Design & Architecture / 艺术设计建筑

**MFA (Master of Fine Arts)**
- Creative Writing / Film / Photography / Theater / Visual Arts / Dance

## Audit Output Contract

When producing a role-specific audit, include:

1. target role direction
2. must-keep entries
3. optional entries
4. weaken/drop entries
5. Skills section keep list
6. Skills section weaken/drop list
7. notes for 1-page CV
8. notes for 2-page CV

## Heuristic

- a strong project bullet does not automatically belong in the Skills section
- a strong skill should usually appear in more than one entry or be critical for the target role
- if a skill is only a one-off implementation detail, keep it inside the project bullet instead of the Skills block
